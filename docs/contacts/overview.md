# Contacts Overview

**Contacts** represent the parties involved in tickets — the organisations, departments, or individuals that complaints are filed with or against.

![Contacts List](/img/screenshots/contacts-index.png)

## Contact Types

| Type | Use For |
|---|---|
| **Authority** | Government bodies, regulators, or statutory authorities |
| **Company** | Private businesses and corporations |
| **Department** | A specific department within a larger organisation |
| **Individual** | A named person |

## Relationship to Tickets

A single contact can be linked to many tickets. When viewing a contact, you can see a paginated list of all tickets filed with that contact.

When a contact is soft-deleted, the contact link on existing tickets is preserved and still shows the archived contact's details.
