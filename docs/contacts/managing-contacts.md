# Managing Contacts

## Editing a Contact

Open a contact and click **Edit**. All fields from the [create form](creating-a-contact.md) are editable. You can also add or remove document attachments from the edit form.

## Tags

Tags on contacts work the same as on tickets — type a tag name and press Enter or comma to add it. Tags help you organise contacts by topic, region, or any other category relevant to your workflow.

## Viewing Linked Tickets

On any contact's detail page, you can see a paginated list of all tickets that have been filed with that contact. Each entry links directly to the ticket.

![Contact Detail](/img/screenshots/contact-show.png)

## Deleting a Contact

Contacts are **soft deleted**. When a contact is deleted:

- They no longer appear in the contacts list or the contact selector on ticket forms.
- Existing tickets that reference the contact still show the contact's name and details (the link is preserved).
