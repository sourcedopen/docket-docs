# Logging In

## Steps

1. Open your browser and navigate to the Open Docket URL provided by your administrator.
2. On the login page, enter your **email address** and **password**.
3. Click **Log In**.

![Login Page](/img/screenshots/login.png)

You will be redirected to your [Dashboard](dashboard.md) upon successful authentication.

## Forgot Your Password?

Use the **Forgot Password** link on the login page to receive a password reset email.
