# Deleting a Ticket

## How to Delete

1. Open the ticket you want to delete.
2. Click **Delete** in the actions bar.
3. Confirm the deletion in the prompt that appears.

## Soft Delete

Tickets are **soft deleted** — they disappear from all ticket lists and search results, but the underlying data is preserved in the database. This means the audit trail and linked records are not permanently destroyed.

:::caution
Deleted tickets cannot be recovered from the UI. Contact your administrator if a ticket needs to be restored.
:::
